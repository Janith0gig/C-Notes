Difference btw Encapsulation and Abstraction:-

Encapsulation:- 

Focus:- how data is hidden inside a class.
Achieved by :- access modifiers.
solves the problem in implementation level. 

Abstraction:-

Focus:- What functionalities are exposed.
Achieved by :- Abstract classes, interfaces, or pure virtual functions.
Solves the problem in design level


What use of member function inside and outside the class? with real time example.
Inside the class is denoted as incline function. and used to simple and to improves performance of the compiler.

Outside the class are denoted by scope resolution (::). and is used for complex functions.

eg:- bank account 
inside :- showing balance
outside:- depositing and withdrawing money
